About

It began in 1979

Kassav’ is a Caribbean band formed in Guadeloupe in 1979. The core members of the band are Jocelyne Béroard, Jacob Desvarieux, Jean-Philippe Marthély, Patrick St. Eloi, Jean-Claude Naimro, Claude Vamur and Georges Décimus (who left to form Volt Face and returned).
Kassav’ have issued over 20 albums, with a further 12 solo albums by band members.
The music of Kassav is an extension of cadence-lypso or compas bands, such as Grammacks, Exile One, Les Aiglons and Experience 7 of the 1970s.
Kassav’ is the creator of the fast carnival zouk style.

40 Years

Big Anniversary Tour 2019

This year Kassav' celebrates their 40th birthday, and they started with a sold-out mega concert on the 11th of May in the La Défense Arena in Paris (capacity 40,000!).
Jacob Desvarieux, Jocelyn Béroard and the other band members still sound fresh and their repertoire is still loved everywhere in the Caribbean, Africa and the rest of the world.
Expect an evening full of the best Caribbean music, big hits, animations and guest artists.
